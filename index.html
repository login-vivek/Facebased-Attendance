How it works — Example
Subject: Mathematics  ·  Start: 09:00  ·  End: 10:00  ·  Grace: 10 min
→ Scan between 09:00 – 09:10 = PRESENT
→ Scan between 09:11 – 10:00 = LATE
→ Scan outside class hours = PRESENT (no active class)
